Medical Image Segmentation

Medical image segmentation is a crucial technique in healthcare that involves partitioning medical images (such as MRI, CT scans, and X-rays) into meaningful regions to assist in disease diagnosis, treatment planning, and research. Using deep learning models like Vision Transformers (ViTs) and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), I have worked on multi-cancer detection by segmenting tumors and abnormalities in medical scans. My approach focuses on improving segmentation accuracy, enabling early detection, and enhancing clinical decision-making.

Key Technologies: Vision Transformers, CNNs, Deep Learning, Image Processing, Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch

E- Commerce Customer Segmentation

Customer segmentation in e-commerce involves categorizing customers into distinct groups based on their purchasing behavior, preferences, and demographics. This enables businesses to personalize marketing strategies, improve customer retention, and optimize product recommendations. In my work, I have leveraged machine learning algorithms such as K-Means Clustering, DBSCAN, and Hierarchical Clustering, along with deep learning techniques, to analyze customer data and uncover meaningful insights. By using features like purchase history, browsing behavior, and customer demographics, my approach helps businesses enhance customer experience and drive revenue growth.

Key Technologies: Python, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch, Data Visualization, Unsupervised Learning
